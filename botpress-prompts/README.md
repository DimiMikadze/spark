# Spark

Spark is enumeral's AI assistant. It talks to leads across every channel — website, WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, and more — figures out whether they're a fit, and books a call with the team, all without a human in the loop.

## What Spark does

**Qualifies leads.** Spark asks a few simple questions about the visitor's business and what they're looking for. Visitors who aren't a good match get a helpful response and move on. Visitors who are get handed off to booking.

**Books meetings.** Spark checks the team's calendar availability in real time, proposes times that work, and creates the calendar event once the visitor confirms.

**Handles timezones.** Spark asks the visitor what city they're in, converts it to the right timezone, and always shows times in their local time — no mental math, no mix-ups between a lead in London and a team in Tbilisi.

**Speaks the visitor's language.** If a visitor writes in Georgian, Spanish, or any other language, Spark replies in that same language throughout the conversation.

**Handles changes.** If a lead comes back to reschedule or cancel, Spark recognizes them and updates the booking — no need to email back and forth.

**Answers questions.** Spark knows enumeral's services, pricing, and how the team works. It can answer common questions on the spot so visitors don't have to wait for a reply.

**Remembers visitors.** When someone returns to the site, Spark picks up where the conversation left off instead of starting over.

**Sends follow-ups.** After every booking, reschedule, or cancellation, Spark emails the team a summary of the conversation, the lead's details, and the agreed time — so there's always a clean record.

## What Spark connects to

Spark is built to fit into the tools a business already uses:

- **Calendar** — Google Calendar (create, update, cancel, check availability)
- **Email** — sends booking confirmations and summaries
- **Database** — stores every lead with their business description, needs, and booking status
- **Knowledge base** — pulls from company documents so answers stay accurate as services and pricing evolve

Spark is built on Botpress, which means it can be extended to connect with CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive), messaging platforms (WhatsApp, Slack, Telegram), and most enterprise systems via API.

## Why it matters

Most leads drop off when they have to wait. Spark replies instantly, 24/7, in the visitor's language, and qualifies them before they ever reach a salesperson. The team only spends time on conversations that are already worth having.
